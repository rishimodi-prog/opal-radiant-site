Our Story

Before Opal, our founders often visited clinics where they felt uncomfortable - male staff around, crowded waiting areas, and no real sense of privacy.

That experience sparked an idea: create a clinic designed entirely for women, where every detail - from the ambience to the staff - puts women first.

Opal Radiant was founded by Dr. Punit Sakhare (Dermatologist), Priyanka, Monica, and Rachana. What started as a boutique studio has since expanded to 4 branches across Mumbai, serving thousands of women who value privacy, safety, and science-backed treatments.

What's in the Name?
