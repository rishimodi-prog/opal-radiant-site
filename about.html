Founder's Story

The Story Behind Opal Radiant

Why Opal was created

Before Opal, our founders often visited clinics where they felt uncomfortable — male staff around, crowded waiting areas, and no real sense of privacy. They realised that if they felt this way, countless other women must be feeling it too. Opal Radiant was born from that discomfort: a women-only space where you never have to feel watched, judged or exposed — just safe, respected and beautifully taken care of.

The founding team

Founded by dermatologist Dr. Punit Sakhare, Priyanka, Monica and Rachana, Opal Radiant brings together strong women leaders with deep experience in beauty, wellness and medical aesthetics.

Growth from one clinic to many

What began as a single boutique studio has grown into four Opal Radiant branches across Mumbai — serving women in Powai, Wadala, Borivali and Thane, while keeping the same personalised, boutique experience at every location.

The Name

What "Opal Radiant" Stands For

Opal = the many shades of a woman — multifaceted, unique, beautiful in every form.

Radiant = natural glow, not change — we enhance what you already have; we don't try to make you someone else.

A clinic that honours your individuality and brings out your most confident, luminous self.
