Frequently Asked Questions

Laser Hair Removal

Is laser hair removal safe for Indian skin?

Yes, when performed with the right laser. We use FDA-approved diode lasers (810nm wavelength) specifically suited for Indian skin tones (Fitzpatrick types IV-V). The diode wavelength penetrates deeper while minimizing surface skin absorption, reducing the risk of burns or hyperpigmentation.

How many sessions do I need for laser hair removal?

Most patients need 6-8 sessions spaced 4-6 weeks apart. This is because hair grows in cycles and the laser only targets hair in the active growth (anagen) phase. After completing the course, most patients see 80-90% permanent hair reduction.

Does laser hair removal hurt?

Most patients describe it as a mild snapping sensation, similar to a rubber band flick. Our lasers have integrated cooling technology that significantly reduces discomfort. Pain decreases with each session as hair becomes finer.

What is the cost of full body laser hair removal?

Full body laser hair removal at OPAL Radiant starts at Rs 6,999 per session. We also offer a 6-session package at Rs 29,999. Visit our pricing page for detailed rates.

Can I get laser hair removal during my period?

Yes, you can undergo laser treatment during menstruation. However, you may experience slightly increased sensitivity. We recommend informing your technician so they can adjust settings if needed.

How should I prepare for my laser hair removal session?

Shave the treatment area 24 hours before your appointment. Avoid waxing, threading, or plucking for 4-6 weeks prior. Stay out of direct sun exposure and discontinue any retinoid creams for a week before treatment. See our pre and post care guide for full details.

Fat Freeze / Cryolipolysis

What is fat freeze and how does it work?

Fat freeze (cryolipolysis) is a non-invasive procedure that uses controlled cooling to freeze and destroy fat cells. Fat cells are more sensitive to cold than surrounding tissue, so they undergo apoptosis (natural cell death) while your skin and muscles remain unharmed. The body naturally eliminates the dead cells over 8-12 weeks.

Is fat freeze a weight loss treatment?

No. Fat freeze is designed for spot reduction of stubborn fat pockets, not overall weight loss. Ideal candidates are near their target weight but have areas of fat that don't respond to diet and exercise -- such as love handles, belly pouch, or thigh fat.

How long before I see fat freeze results?

You may notice early changes at 3-4 weeks. Peak results typically appear at 8-12 weeks, with continued improvement up to 6 months. Most patients see a 20-25% fat reduction per treated area per session.

Are fat freeze results permanent?

Yes, the fat cells destroyed by cryolipolysis are permanently eliminated. However, remaining fat cells can enlarge if you gain significant weight. Maintaining a healthy lifestyle ensures lasting results.

Skin Treatments

What is a carbon laser facial?

A carbon laser facial (also called a Hollywood Peel) involves applying a layer of liquid carbon to the skin, then using an Nd:YAG laser to gently heat and remove it. This deep-cleans pores, reduces oiliness, improves skin texture, and stimulates collagen production -- giving you an instant glow.

How often should I get a hydra facial?

We recommend a hydra facial every 4-6 weeks for optimal results. Each session cleanses, exfoliates, extracts impurities, and hydrates the skin using serums tailored to your skin type.

Which chemical peel is right for my skin?

The right peel depends on your skin concern. Glycolic peels suit dull skin, salicylic peels target acne, and combination peels address pigmentation. During your consultation, Dr. Punit will assess your skin and recommend the most suitable chemical peel type and strength.

What is MNRF and what does it treat?

MNRF (Micro-Needling Radio Frequency) combines tiny needles with radiofrequency energy to stimulate deep collagen production. It effectively treats acne scars, open pores, fine lines, and skin laxity. Most patients need 3-4 sessions spaced 4-6 weeks apart.

Is there any downtime after skin treatments?

Downtime varies by treatment. Hydra facials have zero downtime. Carbon laser facials may cause mild redness for a few hours. Chemical peels can involve 3-5 days of peeling. MNRF may cause redness and mild swelling for 2-3 days. We provide detailed pre and post-care instructions.

HIFU Face Lift

What is HIFU and how does it lift the face?

HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) delivers focused ultrasound energy to the deep structural layers of the skin (SMAS layer), stimulating collagen production and causing tissue tightening. It effectively lifts the jawline, reduces jowls, and tightens the neck and brow area -- all without surgery or injections.

How long do HIFU results last?

HIFU results typically last 12-18 months. You will notice initial tightening immediately, with progressive improvement over 2-3 months as new collagen forms. Annual maintenance sessions can extend the results.

Is HIFU painful?

You may feel warmth and a tingling or prickling sensation during treatment, especially along the jawline. Most patients find it tolerable without any anaesthesia. The discomfort is brief and subsides quickly after the session.

Who is a good candidate for HIFU?

HIFU works best for patients aged 30-65 with mild to moderate skin laxity -- early jowling, softening jawline, or neck looseness. It is not a replacement for a surgical face lift in cases of severe sagging.
