Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Hydra Facial?

A Hydra Facial is a multi-step medical-grade facial treatment that uses patented vortex technology to cleanse, exfoliate, extract impurities, and infuse the skin with hydrating serums. It delivers instant glow, deep hydration, and visibly clearer skin in a single session.

Is the Hydra Facial suitable for all skin types?

Yes, the Hydra Facial is suitable for all skin types, including sensitive, oily, dry, and combination skin. Our dermatologist customises the serums and treatment intensity based on your specific skin concerns.

How long does a Hydra Facial session take?

A standard Hydra Facial session takes approximately 30 to 45 minutes. It is often called a lunchtime facial because there is zero downtime and you can return to your activities immediately.

How often should I get a Hydra Facial?

For best results, we recommend a Hydra Facial every 4 to 6 weeks as a regular skin maintenance routine. For specific concerns like acne or pigmentation, your dermatologist may suggest more frequent initial sessions.

Is there any downtime after the Hydra Facial?

There is absolutely no downtime after a Hydra Facial. Your skin may appear slightly flushed for about 30 minutes, but you can apply makeup and resume normal activities immediately after the session.

Can I get a Hydra Facial before a special event?

Absolutely. The Hydra Facial is one of the best pre-event treatments because it delivers instant glow and hydration with zero downtime. We recommend scheduling your session 1 to 3 days before the event for optimal radiance.
