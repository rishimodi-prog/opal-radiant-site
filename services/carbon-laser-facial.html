Q-Switched Nd:YAG Technology

Our Carbon Facial uses a Q-Switched Nd:YAG laser — a medical-grade, dermatologist-approved laser known for its safety on Indian skin tones. A thin layer of medical-grade liquid carbon is applied to your skin. The Q-Switched Nd:YAG laser then vaporises the carbon along with impurities, dead cells and excess oil while stimulating collagen underneath.

Because the Q-Switch pulse is extremely fast, energy is delivered without damaging surrounding tissue — making it one of the safest lasers for Indian complexions.

Painless & Lunchtime-Friendly

The Carbon Facial is virtually painless — most women describe a warm, tingling sensation like a mild sunburn brush. No anaesthesia is required. A cooling airflow keeps the skin comfortable throughout the session.

Sessions take about 30–45 minutes — a perfect lunchtime facial. There is no visible peeling, no scabbing and no need to hide indoors. You can apply makeup and return to work or social plans immediately.

Risks, Side Effects & Suitability

Possible Side Effects

Carbon Laser Facial is generally well tolerated, but you should be aware of possible short-term effects:

  • Mild warmth, redness or tingling during and after the session (resolves within hours)
  • Very mild sensitivity for 24-48 hours
  • Rarely: temporary pigment changes, especially in deeper skin tones

Serious adverse events are uncommon. All procedures are performed under sterile conditions by trained medical staff under dermatologist supervision.

Who Should Avoid This Treatment

Carbon Laser Facial is not recommended if you are:

  • Pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Active skin infection, herpes outbreak or open wound in the area
  • Recent tan or sunburn (within 2 weeks)
  • Taking isotretinoin (Roaccutane) in the last 6 months
  • History of light-triggered conditions (e.g. photosensitive lupus)

Your dermatologist will review your full medical history during the free consultation before recommending treatment.
