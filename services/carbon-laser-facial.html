How the Treatment Works

1

Consultation

Our dermatologist assesses your skin type, concerns, and medical history to confirm you are an ideal candidate for the carbon laser facial.

2

Carbon Mask Application

A thin layer of medical-grade liquid carbon is applied evenly across your face. It penetrates deep into the pores, binding to oil and impurities.

3

Laser Treatment

The Q-Switch laser is passed over the skin, vaporising the carbon along with dead skin cells, blackheads, and contaminants trapped in your pores.

4

Soothe & Protect

A calming serum and SPF sunscreen are applied to soothe the skin and protect it from UV exposure. You leave with an instant, healthy glow.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a carbon laser facial?

A carbon laser facial is a laser treatment where liquid carbon is applied to the skin, and then a Q-Switch laser vaporises it along with dead skin cells, excess oil, and impurities. The result is deeply cleansed, smoother, brighter, and more even-toned skin.

Is the carbon laser facial painful?

Most clients experience only a mild tingling sensation during the treatment. The procedure is very well tolerated and does not require any anaesthesia.

How many sessions are needed for best results?

For optimal results, we recommend 4 to 6 sessions spaced 2 to 3 weeks apart. Your dermatologist will create a personalised treatment plan based on your specific skin concerns and goals.

Is there any downtime after the treatment?

There is zero downtime after a carbon laser facial. You can resume your normal activities, apply makeup, and go about your day immediately after the session.

Is the carbon laser facial safe for Indian skin?

Yes, the carbon laser facial is specifically safe for darker skin tones, including Indian skin. The Q-Switch laser is well-suited for all Fitzpatrick skin types commonly found in South Asia, and our dermatologist customises settings for your skin type.

How long does the treatment take?

A typical carbon laser facial session takes approximately 30 to 45 minutes, including preparation and post-treatment care.

Can the carbon laser facial help with acne scars?

Yes, the carbon laser facial helps reduce the appearance of acne scars over multiple sessions by stimulating collagen production and promoting skin cell renewal.

How soon will I see results?

You will notice an instant glow and smoother skin texture after your very first session. Results improve progressively with each subsequent treatment.

What should I avoid after the treatment?

After a carbon laser facial, avoid direct sun exposure and harsh skincare products for at least 48 hours. Apply a broad-spectrum SPF 50 sunscreen when stepping outdoors. Your dermatologist will provide detailed post-care instructions.
