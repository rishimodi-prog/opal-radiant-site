MNRF — Microneedling Radiofrequency

For Acne Scars, Open Pores & Skin Tightening

Book Free Consultation WhatsApp Us

Why Choose MNRF?

🎯

Acne Scar Reduction

RF energy delivered deep into the dermis triggers intensive collagen remodelling, filling in and smoothing out pitted acne scars, boxcar scars, and rolling scars over multiple sessions.

✨

Skin Tightening

The radiofrequency heat contracts existing collagen fibres and stimulates new collagen formation, resulting in firmer, tighter skin with improved elasticity and a lifted appearance.

🔍

Pore Refinement

The thermal effect of RF energy tightens the walls of enlarged pores from within, visibly reducing pore size and giving the skin a smoother, more refined texture.

☀

Fine Line Softening

Boosted collagen and elastin production plumps the skin from within, softening fine lines and early wrinkles for a more youthful, rejuvenated complexion.

How the Treatment Works

1

Consultation

Our dermatologist examines your skin, assesses scar depth, and discusses your goals to determine the ideal MNRF settings and treatment plan for your skin type.

2

Numbing

A topical anaesthetic cream is applied to the treatment area 30 to 45 minutes before the procedure to ensure you are comfortable throughout the session.

3

RF Microneedling

The MNRF device is passed over the skin, delivering insulated radiofrequency energy through fine needles into the deeper layers of the dermis, stimulating collagen regeneration.

4

Aftercare

A soothing serum and broad-spectrum sunscreen are applied. You will receive detailed aftercare instructions to support healing and maximise your results.

Who Is It For?

MNRF is ideal for anyone looking to address these common skin concerns:

Acne Scars

If you have pitted, boxcar, or rolling acne scars that have not responded to topical treatments, MNRF offers deep collagen remodelling for visible scar improvement.

Open & Enlarged Pores

Enlarged pores on the cheeks and nose respond well to the thermal tightening effect of RF energy, giving the skin a smoother, more polished appearance.

Skin Laxity & Fine Lines

Early signs of aging, mild skin laxity, and fine lines around the eyes, mouth, and forehead are effectively treated with collagen-boosting RF energy.

Uneven Skin Texture

Rough, bumpy skin texture caused by scarring, sun damage, or aging is progressively smoothed out with each MNRF session as new collagen forms.

Stretch Marks

MNRF can also help improve the appearance of stretch marks on the body by stimulating collagen renewal in the affected areas over multiple sessions.

Results Timeline

After 1 Session

Skin feels tighter and smoother within a week as initial collagen contraction takes effect. Mild redness subsides within 1 to 2 days post-treatment.

After 2–3 Sessions

Visible improvement in acne scars and pore size. Skin texture becomes noticeably more even. New collagen continues to form for several weeks after each session.

After 4–6 Sessions

Significant scar reduction, firmer skin, and refined pores. Maximum collagen remodelling is achieved. Results continue to improve for up to 3 months after the final session.

Downtime & Important Notes

Expected Downtime

Mild redness and slight swelling are normal for 1 to 2 days after the procedure. Tiny pinpoint marks may be visible for 24 to 48 hours. Most clients return to work the following day. Avoid direct sun exposure and apply SPF 50 sunscreen diligently for at least one week.

Contraindications

MNRF is not suitable for individuals with active skin infections, keloid tendency, pregnancy or breastfeeding, uncontrolled diabetes, cardiac pacemakers, or those on blood-thinning medications. Your dermatologist will assess your suitability during the initial consultation.
