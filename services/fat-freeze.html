How Fat Freeze Works

1

Freeze

A specialised applicator is placed on the target area, cooling fat cells to their crystallisation point. Surrounding skin, nerves, and muscles remain unharmed.

2

Body Clears

Over the following weeks, your body naturally processes and eliminates the dead fat cells through its lymphatic system. No effort required on your part.

3

Lasting Results

Treated fat cells are gone permanently. You will see a noticeable reduction in fat bulges, revealing a slimmer, more contoured figure.

Are You an Ideal Candidate?

Fat freeze is ideal for individuals who are close to their target weight but struggle with stubborn pockets of fat that resist diet and exercise. It is not a weight-loss procedure but rather a body contouring treatment.

You may be a good candidate if:

  • You have noticeable fat bulges in specific areas
  • You are within a healthy weight range
  • You want a non-surgical alternative to liposuction
  • You are looking for permanent fat reduction
  • You prefer zero downtime after treatment

During your free consultation, our dermatologist will assess your concerns and determine if fat freeze is the right treatment for you.

Most clients experience a mild pulling and cooling sensation during the procedure. The treatment is well-tolerated and does not require anaesthesia. Any initial discomfort typically subsides within the first few minutes as the area becomes numb.
