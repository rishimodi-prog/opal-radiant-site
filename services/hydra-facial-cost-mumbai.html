What Determines Your Hydra Facial Cost?

Every client is different. Here are the factors our dermatologist reviews to give you an honest, personalised quote.

Session type

Signature vs deluxe (with LED, boosters) — deluxe costs more.

Frequency

Monthly subscriptions cost less per session than à la carte.

Booster serums

Personalised serums (peptides, brightening, anti-ageing) add cost.

Add-ons

LED therapy, extractions, or lymphatic drainage add per-session cost.

Clinic vs salon

Medical-grade Hydra Facial at a dermatologist clinic costs more but delivers deeper results.
