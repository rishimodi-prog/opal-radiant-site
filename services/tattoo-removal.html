Our 5-Step Tattoo Removal Process

A structured, dermatologist-supervised approach designed for safety and results on Indian skin.

1

Consultation

Our dermatologist evaluates your tattoo's size, colour, depth, and skin type to create a personalised treatment plan with realistic expectations.

2

Patch Test

A small test area is treated to assess your skin's response to the laser and determine the optimal energy settings for safe removal.

3

Numbing

A topical numbing cream is applied 30-45 minutes before the procedure to minimise discomfort and ensure a comfortable experience.

4

Laser Treatment

The Q-switched laser delivers targeted pulses that break down tattoo pigment into tiny particles, which your body naturally eliminates over time.

5

Aftercare

You receive detailed aftercare instructions including wound care, sun protection, and follow-up scheduling. Sessions are spaced 6-8 weeks apart for optimal healing.

Tattoo Fading Timeline

Results vary based on tattoo size, colour, and skin type. Here is a general timeline of what to expect.

🌑

1–2 Sessions

Early lightening becomes visible. The tattoo starts to look faded and the ink begins breaking down beneath the skin surface.

🌓

3–5 Sessions

Significant fading of 50–70% is typically achieved. The tattoo becomes noticeably lighter with each successive session.

🌕

6+ Sessions

Nearly invisible results for most tattoos. Black and dark inks often achieve near-complete removal with continued treatment.
